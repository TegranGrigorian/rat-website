What is RAT?

RAT (Rust Archive Tool) is a straightforward command-line utility designed for compressing and decompressing files and folders using the .tar.gz format. Built with Rust, it prioritizes simplicity, speed, and scriptability‚Äîremoving the confusion of traditional tar commands.

Why Choose RAT?

  • No learning curve: Tar syntax is infamous. RAT keeps it simple.
  • Written in Rust: Fast, safe, and memory-efficient.
  • Automation-friendly: Perfect for scripts and CI/CD pipelines.
  • Cross-platform: Works on Linux, macOS, and Windows.
