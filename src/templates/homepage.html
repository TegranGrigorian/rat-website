Quick Start

Get started with RAT in seconds ‚Äî just a couple example commands to compress and extract.

Compress a folder

rat input_folder

Creates input_folder.tar.gz

Compress with custom output

rat input_folder my-archive.tar.gz

Creates my-archive.tar.gz

Extract an archive

rat -d archive.tar.gz

Extracts to archive folder

Compress and delete original

rat -d input_folder backup.tar.gz

Compresses and removes input_folder

Compress to tar.xz!

rat -x input_folder backup.tar.xz

Compresses to input_folder.tar.xz

Verbose help flag

rat --help
